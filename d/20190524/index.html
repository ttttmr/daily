我尝试了一下i have a zero-day

得到了以下内容，顺便用Google翻译了一下，好像还不错

i have a zero-day vulnerability for all of Google’s OSes.” Apple is already vulnerable to a zero-day vulnerability for Android, according to Reuters. “The company is in the process of investigating security issues on all of its devices.”

我对所有Google操作系统都有零日漏洞。据路透社报道，苹果已经容易受到Android零日漏洞的影响。 “该公司正在调查其所有设备的安全问题。”

Google is apparently testing a fix for the issue, and at the end of June announced it would support all OS’ from Android to iOS. That may sound like a big deal for new customers, but if you are a consumer who might be worried about future vulnerabilities, this makes little sense.

谷歌显然正在测试该问题的解决方案，并在6月底宣布它将支持从Android到iOS的所有操作系统。这听起来对新客户来说意义重大，但如果你是一个可能担心未来漏洞的消费者，这没什么意义。

Android devices, while widely embraced by smartphone enthusiasts, do pose an unique threat to security if they fall victim to one. Because they are mobile software, the Android OS is generally more vulnerable to vulnerabilities. This makes Google Android the largest target. Google’s Android devices are still widely embraced, but the new vulnerability could result in significant traffic for the operating systems of those customers.

Android设备虽然受到智能手机爱好者的广泛欢迎，但如果成为受害者，则会对安全造成独特的威胁。由于它们是移动软件，因此Android操作系统通常更容易受到漏洞的攻击。这使得谷歌Android成为最大的目标。谷歌的Android设备仍然被广泛接受，但新漏洞可能会导致这些客户的操作系统出现大量流量。

To the extent people use Android apps or services to attack Google’s OSes, Google is actually better off doing so for Android itself. Google is still selling Android to hardware makers like Dell, Acer, Microsoft, Hewlett-Packard, and other Android device makers in order to deliver the highest-margin product line.

在某种程度上，人们使用Android应用程序或服务攻击谷歌的操作系统，谷歌实际上更适合Android本身。谷歌仍在向戴尔，宏基，微软，惠普和其他Android设备制造商等硬件制造商销售Android，以提供利润率最高的产品线。
